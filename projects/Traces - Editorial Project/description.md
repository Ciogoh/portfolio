**Traces - A visual research on movement, paths, and the marks they leave.**
Every movement leaves a trace. Sometimes it becomes a path, a line carved by repetition, by choosing the same way again and again. Other times it appears as a mark: a momentary gesture, a curve drawn in passing, almost disappearing as soon as it appears

This book looks at both. At the terrains that shape how we move. At the paths that emerge because we cross them.
